About the need for bicycle measurements

Professional road riders competing at the world tour pay great attention to detail to their equipment choice because a rider’s position on the bike has a major contribution towards the athlete’s performance by making the rider more aerodynamic and comfortable. Riders would optimize their riding position -reducing the aerodynamic drag- in a wind tunnel to suit the changing frame geometries from year to year and with different manufacturers. There are many unique benefits of using a cycling wind tunnel for the ability to accurately simulate real world cycling scenarios and measure cycling drag can help the rider adjust their position for both aerodynamic performance and comfort.

Depending on the physical ability of different riders, they might not be able to perform at their best in a very aerodynamic position. Bike fitting is the science of reaching that balance between comfort and aerodynamics. From race to race, bikes would get disassembled to save space in transport. Further, when riders choose to change components -saddles, handlebars- for different courses, team mechanics has to make those changes. This will be important for professional road riders and take value for the biggest sport consumer markets. If they easily and accurately measure the critical key measurements using a less complicated tool, it will be beneficial to professional riders.

In the experience of professional riders, small effects such as pedal type and position, seat treatment, and misalignments can all affect drag measurements of a bicycle .This is important when comparing the relative performance of different bicycles. Some measurements were arranged according to their riders’.

The Riders’ position on a bicycle is aerodynamically optimized. Bike aerodynamics is considered to be one of the most complex applications in aerodynamics not only because of the bluff body shape of the rider, which generates the majority of the drag but also due to the transient cycling movement. Therefore when a rider’s position is optimized the following key measurements are important. In this application use the below mentioned key measurements.

Key measurements
  • Saddle height: Centre of the bottom bracket to top of the saddle
  • Reach: tip of the saddle to center of the handlebars

When considering the key measurements, it is important to reason about key measurements. The saddle height is measured along the seat tube from the top of the saddle to the middle of the bottom bracket spindle. Saddle height is important for both power generation and accident prevention. And another main measurement is reach. That is the distance between the tip of the saddle and the middle of the handlebar. The reach, like the handlebar height, is influenced by flexibility and comfort. The higher the aerodynamics, the more spread out. However, the trade-off is that the hip angle is also near, which inhibits power generation.

Finally, the main goal of this was to use the phone camera to measure the key measurements. Existing tools give the most accurate results with more specific features. But those were complex mechanisms with weighted tools. Those are difficult to use for day-to-day needs. Therefore, to solve these problems would be to use a phone camera then use image processing techniques and machine learning with computer vision techniques to calibrate the key measurements.
